1. At first respective company will have to send letter to the TBL authority stating what particular tasks they want to be done.

2. After that, the company needs to share their document check list with TBL. i.e. draft agreement has to be shared between two companies.

3. After the completion of draft agreement sharing, the agreement paper will be signed between TBL and the respective company. All the terms & conditions along with the detailed Information will be clearly stated in that paper.

4. Later, requirements are finalized.TBL will clearly mention the services they will be providing under the agreement as well as what remuneration they will get from the project.

5. TBL authority will clearly state the project containing programs such as: application form, roll no generation, admit card, SMS generation.

6. After the programs have done, TBL fixes up a ‚ÄúTEST‚Äù procedure for checking purpose i.e., TBL authority ensures the complete accuracy of the project before handover.

7. Following the success of the ‚ÄúTEST‚Äù procedure, publicity is done by both companies according to their own policy. If agreement is done for add. publishing, TBL will take the obligation for necessary publicity arrangements.

8. Afterwards, a period of time is required for handling the admit card along with the attendance sheet. Moreover, automated roll generation, seating arrangements, payment option etc. are to be completed during this period. Basically this time is required for the applicants and TBL monitor the whole process on behalf of the company.

9. After the completion of all the steps, TBL will deduct the amount as per agreement. The remaining total collected amount received from the applicants will be sent to the company. And finally, as per agreement,TBL will handover the data to the respective company they are working for.
